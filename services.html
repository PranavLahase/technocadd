Advanced 3D Printing & Additive Manufacturing

Multi-technology 3D printing services for rapid prototyping, functional testing, tooling, and low-volume production using FDM, SLA, SLS (polymer), and metal additive manufacturing.

Technologies & Materials

FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling)

PLA, ABS, PETG, Nylon, TPU, carbon fiber composites. Ideal for functional prototypes, fixtures, and end-use parts.

SLA (Stereolithography)

High-resolution resin printing for detailed models, master patterns, injection mold prototypes, and dental/medical applications.

SLS (Selective Laser Sintering)

Nylon PA12, PA11, glass-filled polymers for durable functional parts, snap-fits, living hinges, and complex assemblies.

Metal 3D Printing

Aluminum, stainless steel, titanium, Inconel for aerospace, automotive, and medical implants with conformal cooling channels.

Post-Processing Services

  • ✓ Sanding, polishing, and smoothing
  • ✓ Painting, anodizing, and powder coating
  • ✓ Vapor smoothing for resin parts
  • ✓ Insert installation and assembly
