Our Hiring Process

A transparent and efficient recruitment process designed to find the best talent.

01

Application Review

Our HR team reviews all applications within 5-7 business days and shortlists candidates based on qualifications and experience.

02

Phone Screening

Shortlisted candidates receive a call to discuss their background, expectations, and initial fit for the role.

03

Technical Interview
