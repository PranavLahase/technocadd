Who We Are

Transforming Ideas Into Reality

PSP TechnoCADD Ancillary Private Limited is an ISO 9001:2015 certified engineering solutions provider specializing in academic lab development, electric vehicle training systems, CAD design, advanced 3D printing, and manufacturing support services.

Since 2010, we've partnered with universities, technical institutions, automotive manufacturers, aerospace companies, and industrial enterprises to deliver precision-engineered solutions that bridge the gap between design and production.

Our multidisciplinary team of mechanical engineers, CAD designers, electrical specialists, and automation experts brings together deep industry knowledge and cutting-edge technology to solve complex engineering challenges.
