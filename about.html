Crafting Tomorrow's Innovations Today

14+ years of excellence in academic lab development, EV training systems, and precision engineering solutions.

Who We Are

Founded in 2012, PSP TechnoCADD Ancillary Private Limited specializes in technological training systems and engineering solutions across multiple industries, including Robotics, Electric Vehicles (EVs), Industrial Automation, ADAS, Process Instrumentation, Industry 4.0, Drones, and Medical Devices.

We are ISO 9001:2015 certified and recognized for delivering industry-academia integrated solutions. Our complete lab setups, hands-on training systems, and cutting-edge equipment prepare students and professionals for the future of technology.

With headquarters in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar and Pune, Maharashtra, and a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility at Chikalthana MIDC, we serve educational institutions, research organizations, and industrial clients across India.
