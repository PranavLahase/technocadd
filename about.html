Our Process

How We Work

A structured, client-focused workflow that delivers results on time and within scope.

1

Define Requirements

We start with a detailed discovery session to understand your goals, constraints, manufacturing processes, and timeline. Clear inputs = clear outputs.

2

Design & Engineer

CAD modeling, GD&T application, DFM/DfAM reviews, simulations (if needed), and design iterations based on your feedback.

3

Validate & Review

Internal QA checks, client review sessions, tolerance validation, and interference detection before final delivery.

4

Deliver Documentation

Export-ready CAD files, manufacturing drawings (PDF + DWG), BOMs, assembly instructions, and revision-controlled packages.

5

Support & Iterate

Post-delivery support for fabrication queries, design modifications, and production feedback integration.

6

Continuous Improvement

We learn from every project — capturing lessons learned and refining our processes for even better outcomes.
